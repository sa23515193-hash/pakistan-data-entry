Overview

Pakistan’s energy sector includes thermal, hydro, nuclear, and renewable sources. Energy production and consumption are crucial for industry, agriculture, and households.

Energy Production by Source (Approx. 2025):

  • Thermal: 55%
  • Hydropower: 30%
  • Renewable (Solar/Wind): 10%
  • Nuclear: 5%

Energy Consumption by Sector:

  • Industry: 40%
  • Residential: 35%
  • Transport: 15%
  • Other: 10%

Key Insights:

  • Punjab and Sindh have the largest share of power plants.
  • Government encourages renewable energy projects to reduce dependence on fossil fuels.
  • Energy distribution challenges remain in rural and remote areas.
