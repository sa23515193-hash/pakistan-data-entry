Overview

Pakistan has a growing population, with urban areas expanding rapidly. Population distribution varies across provinces, and youth population forms a significant percentage of the total.

Population by Province (Approximate 2025):

  • Punjab: 110 million
  • Sindh: 50 million
  • Khyber Pakhtunkhwa: 35 million
  • Balochistan: 12 million
  • Gilgit-Baltistan: 2 million

Urban vs Rural Population:

  • Urban: 38%
  • Rural: 62%

Key Insights:

  • Punjab is the most populous province with major urban centers.
  • Rural population dominates in Balochistan and Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.
  • Youth population (~60%) offers potential for workforce and digital economy growth.
