Overview

Pakistan's workforce is distributed across various sectors including agriculture, industry, and services. The employment trends vary between urban and rural areas.

Employment by Sector:

  • Agriculture: 38%
  • Industry: 20%
  • Services: 42%

Urban vs Rural Employment:

  • Urban: 55%
  • Rural: 45%

Key Insights:

  • Major employment in agriculture is in Punjab and Sindh.
  • Services sector is growing rapidly in urban areas, especially IT and finance.
  • Government initiatives aim to improve employment in rural and industrial sectors.
