Overview

Pakistan has a diverse industrial sector, including textiles, agriculture-based industries, manufacturing, and IT services. Exports play a major role in the economy.

Major Industries by Province:

  • Punjab: Textiles, Sugar, Cement, Pharmaceuticals
  • Sindh: Textiles, IT Services, Port-based Trade
  • Khyber Pakhtunkhwa: Cement, Marble, Fruits Processing
  • Balochistan: Minerals, Fruits, Livestock Products

Export Share by Product:

  • Textiles: 50%
  • Rice: 15%
  • Leather: 10%
  • Sports Goods: 5%
  • Other: 20%

Key Insights:

  • Punjab dominates textile production and export.
  • Sindh contributes heavily via Karachi ports.
  • Government incentives encourage industrial growth and export diversification.
