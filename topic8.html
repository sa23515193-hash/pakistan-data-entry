Overview

Agriculture is a major sector in Pakistan's economy, providing employment and contributing significantly to GDP. Key crops include wheat, rice, sugarcane, cotton, and maize.

Major Crops Production by Province (Approx. 2025):

  • Punjab: Wheat, Sugarcane, Cotton
  • Sindh: Rice, Cotton, Sugarcane
  • Khyber Pakhtunkhwa: Wheat, Maize, Fruits
  • Balochistan: Wheat, Fruits, Vegetables

Crop Type Share:

  • Wheat: 40%
  • Rice: 20%
  • Sugarcane: 15%
  • Cotton: 15%
  • Other: 10%

Key Insights:

  • Punjab is the leading province in wheat and sugarcane production.
  • Sindh specializes in rice cultivation and cotton.
  • Government supports agriculture through subsidies, modern irrigation, and crop insurance programs.
