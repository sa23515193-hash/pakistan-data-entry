Overview

Pakistan’s infrastructure and transport sector includes road networks, railways, airports, and seaports, essential for economic growth and connectivity.

Major Transport Networks by Province:

  • Punjab: Motorways, Railways, Airports (Lahore, Faisalabad)
  • Sindh: Karachi Ports, Motorways, Railways
  • Khyber Pakhtunkhwa: National Highways, Railways, BRT Systems
  • Balochistan: Quetta Roads, Gwadar Port

Transport Mode Share:

  • Road: 60%
  • Rail: 25%
  • Air: 10%
  • Sea: 5%

Key Insights:

  • Punjab and Sindh have the most developed road and rail networks.
  • Gwadar port development boosts Balochistan’s trade potential.
  • Government initiatives focus on expanding highways, urban mass transit, and modernizing airports.
