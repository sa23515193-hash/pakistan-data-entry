Overview

Pakistan’s technology and innovation sector is growing rapidly with startups, IT services, and R&D contributing to economic growth.

Tech Startups by Province (Approx. 2025):

  • Punjab: 60%
  • Sindh: 25%
  • Khyber Pakhtunkhwa: 10%
  • Balochistan: 5%

IT Sector Share:

  • Software Development: 50%
  • IT Services: 30%
  • Digital Marketing: 10%
  • Other Tech: 10%

Key Insights:

  • Lahore and Karachi are the major hubs for startups and IT development.
  • Government supports innovation through tech incubators and digital transformation policies.
  • Emerging technologies like AI, fintech, and e-commerce are gaining momentum.
