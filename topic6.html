Overview

Pakistan's healthcare system includes public and private hospitals, clinics, and community health centers. The number of doctors, nurses, and paramedics varies across provinces.

Number of Hospitals by Province (Approx. 2025):

  • Punjab: 1,200
  • Sindh: 600
  • Khyber Pakhtunkhwa: 450
  • Balochistan: 200
  • Gilgit-Baltistan: 50

Healthcare Workers Distribution:

  • Doctors: 35%
  • Nurses: 40%
  • Paramedics: 25%

Key Insights:

  • Major hospitals concentrated in urban areas like Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad.
  • Rural areas face challenges with access to healthcare professionals.
  • Government initiatives focus on improving health infrastructure and disease control.
