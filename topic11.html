Overview

Pakistan has a large youth population, forming around 60% of the total population. Youth are involved in education, employment, entrepreneurship, and online work.

Age Distribution:

  • 0-14 years: 35%
  • 15-24 years: 25%
  • 25-34 years: 20%
  • 35+ years: 20%

Youth Participation:

  • In Education: 55%
  • In Employment: 30%
  • In Online Work: 15%

Key Insights:

  • Major youth population in Punjab and Sindh.
  • Growing trend of entrepreneurship and freelancing among youth.
  • Government programs promoting skill development and education for youth.
