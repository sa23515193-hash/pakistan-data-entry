Overview

Pakistan maintains a robust defense and security structure, including the army, navy, air force, and paramilitary forces.

Military Personnel by Province (Approx. 2025):

  • Punjab: 50%
  • Sindh: 20%
  • Khyber Pakhtunkhwa: 20%
  • Balochistan: 10%

Defense Budget Allocation:

  • Army: 60%
  • Air Force: 20%
  • Navy: 15%
  • Other Security Forces: 5%

Key Insights:

  • Punjab hosts major military training centers and command headquarters.
  • Security is strategically managed across borders and internal regions.
  • Government focuses on modernization, defense technology, and disaster management readiness.
