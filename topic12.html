Overview

Pakistan’s economy is diverse with contributions from agriculture, industry, and services sectors. Trade includes both exports and imports of goods and services.

GDP Contribution by Sector:

  • Agriculture: 20%
  • Industry: 25%
  • Services: 55%

Trade Balance (Approx. 2025):

  • Exports: 30%
  • Imports: 70%

Key Insights:

  • Services sector dominates GDP, including IT, finance, and trade services.
  • Textiles and agriculture products are major exports.
  • Government is working on improving trade balance via export promotion and import substitution policies.
